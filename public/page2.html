Education 4.0 Pedagogies

NEXGEN’s Industry 4.0 Tech courses are delivered through
Education 4.0 methodologies integrated with AI to personalize the
learning experience & maximize learning outcomes

AI Personalized Tutor

Students are accompanied with a Personalized AI tutor available 24/7, providing instant, accurate answers, explains complex topics in a simple manner, step-by-step guidance, Provides examples, extra practice questions with feedback on answers all in a natural conversation available in multiple languages

Project Based learning

Our ‘learn by doing’ approach immerses students in Industry 4.0 tech projects, equipping them with specialized kits to build tech solutions, addressing environmental challenges while fostering key industry skills and eco-innovation.

AI-Powered Competency Based Education

AI-powered learning experience platform (LXP) enables CBD. Its self-paced approach prioritizes student mastery over a one-size-fits-all method. Students’ progress based on their demonstrated understanding through AI assessments and rubrics, ensuring deep learning before moving forward.

Animated Classes

Students are accompanied with a Personalized We use vibrant, engaging animations to simplify complex concepts into clear, visually captivating content that speaks to young digital natives. Our animations open a gateway to an adventurous learning experience, keeping students absorbed and eager to learn.

AI Assessment

Students get personalized AI feedback on their answers, providing valuable insights, AI identifies areas to improve, explains incorrect answers, clarifies misconception and highlights strengths. AI transforms assessment into a journey of growth instead of just a grade.

Performance based assessment

Assessment method tailored for the 4th industrial revolution, centers on hands-on project assessments that build practical skills and prepare students for industry realities. This method enhances technical abilities, problem-solving, and critical thinking, key for success in the modern workplace

Formative assessments

AI-powered LXP platform enables Formative assessments, instead of end-of-course exams, students engage in quizzes, discussions, and assignments at each level of their learning. This provides immediate feedback, pinpointing areas for improvement and empowering students to actively grow their understanding.

Digital Portfolios (SCV)

Our digital portfolio system gives students a powerful tool to showcase their accomplishments. Each portfolio documents mastered Industry 4.0 tech, Skills unlocked, completed capstone projects, and serves as a record of their expertise, preparing them to succeed in the professional world.
